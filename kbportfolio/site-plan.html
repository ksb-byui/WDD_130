Overview

Purpose

For my personal project, I am planning to make a multi-purpose Personal Portfolio Website. The primary purpose of my website will be to link to my resume to provide additional information about me, as well as a repository of projects I have worked on and definitely want potential employers to see. I'd possibly include a link/links to another site that could host other pet projects that I don't necessarily want to advertise to the employers but that I wouldn't mind having them see if they were interested.

Audience

My intended audience is interviewers and potential employers.

Content

Home page

This will be the page that the user lands on when clicking my website's link. As it may be a potential employer that would be landing here, I want to keep it quite simple and professional while displaying an obvious path to my "For Employers" page, but also having a path to personal projects that I am proud of and that demonstrate my skills in case the employers would be interested in seeing these, or if a general user wants access to my open source projects. This page will include: Simple navigation bar with logo, "contact me," "about me" paragraph, photo of me, my "why?" statement, footer with links to social media/professional media.

Images for the Home page

For Employers

This page will be no nonsense access to information that would be important to a potential employer such as work experience, certifications, licensure, unofficial transcript, references, etc. I'm undecided wether to make it a display of my resume formatted to fit a screen or to have a link to my resume document, unofficial transcript, linkedin, etc. Regardless, I'll likely include simple photos that act as dividers for the information.

Images for the Page 2

Personal Projects

This page will be available to anyone who accesses my home page. It will mainly be a place where I can show off my coolest projects but it could also be a place for employers to get an idea of what skills I have and how I've applied them. It can also be a place for interested people to access my 3D files or code to copy and use or edit to their liking. I could also do write-ups on the projects and provide links to equipment or materials I used.
